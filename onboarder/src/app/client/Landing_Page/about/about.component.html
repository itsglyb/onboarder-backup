Onboarder offers you Events and Membership Management. Our platform is designed to simplify and streamline these critical aspects of your organization or community.

About us

Events Management

Events management system is developed to automate the manual event handling processes. This will encompass various features and functionalities tailored to the needs of events announcement and registration and handling of the organizations. These functionalities include event creation and organization, registration or ticketing, schedule management, and analytics.

Membership Management

Membership management system on the other hand is developed to automate the manual membership registration process that can be accessed through the website. The functionalities of this system are member registration and renewal, member payment verification, membership validation, membership analytics, and integration of event management functionalities that allows verified members to register for events, view its details.
